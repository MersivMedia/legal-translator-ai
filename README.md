# Legal Simple AI 🏛️

Transform complex legal documents into plain English instantly with AI.

## 🌟 Features

- **AI-Powered Simplification**: Advanced AI translates legal jargon into understandable language
- **Key Points Extraction**: Automatically identifies the most important points  
- **Risk Analysis**: Highlights potential concerns and risks you should know about
- **Free to Use**: 3 document simplifications per day, completely free
- **No Signup Required**: Start using immediately
- **Mobile Friendly**: Works perfectly on all devices
- **Secure Processing**: Documents are not stored permanently

## 📄 Supported Document Types

- Employment Contracts
- Rental Agreements  
- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policies
- NDAs & Confidentiality Agreements
- Service Contracts
- Loan Documents
- Insurance Policies
- Purchase Agreements
- Partnership Contracts

## ⚖️ Important Disclaimer

Legal Simple AI is an educational tool that helps you understand legal documents. **It does not provide legal advice.** Always consult with a qualified attorney for legal decisions and advice.

## 🚀 Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript
- **Backend**: Node.js with Vercel serverless functions
- **AI**: OpenAI GPT-4o-mini
- **Deployment**: Vercel
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics
- **Monetization**: Google AdSense

## 💰 Revenue Model

- Google AdSense advertisements
- Strategic ad placement for optimal user experience
- Rate limiting encourages return visits
